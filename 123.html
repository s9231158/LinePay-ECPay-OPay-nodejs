交易失敗 Transaction failed

訊息代碼 Message code：10100050
訊息說明 Note for message：Parameter Error. Not In Spec.

常見失敗原因如下 Frequently seen causes for failure as below

  • 商店尚未開啟收款服務，請聯繫商店處理。 The merchant has not activated payment collection service.

  • 交易金額低於下限或超過上限
    Transaction is less than lower limit or more than upper limit.

  • 此商店未提供任何付款方式，請聯繫商店處理。 The store does not provide any payment method. Please contact the merchant.

  • 此商店所傳送串接參數錯誤，請聯繫商店處理。 Incorrect parameter. Please contact the merchant.

  • 建議您先與購買商品的商家或購物網站業者聯繫 Suggest contact with seller of the merchandize or online shop first!

若您的問題非以上原因，請洽綠界客服中心，我們將協助處理您所遇到的問題。
Please contact ECPay customer service if you have checked and confirmed the above issues do not exist. We will assist you with the problem.
